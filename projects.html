Projects

Secure Infrastructure for Agentic Systems

Designing the underlying platforms, protocols, and trust boundaries that let autonomous agents act on behalf of users without exceeding their authority.

  • SAGA
  • MAGIQ
  • SAGA-BFT

Web Agent Security

Studying how language-model agents that browse and act on the web can be misled, hijacked, or otherwise driven toward unintended behavior — and how to constrain them.

  • MUZZLE

Defenses for Agentic Systems

Developing detection, containment, and recovery mechanisms for multi-step agentic pipelines, with the goal of catching misbehavior before it causes irreversible action.

  • ACE

Multi-Agent Systems

Examining security and coordination challenges that arise when multiple agents interact, including adversarial agents embedded in otherwise honest populations.

  • APWA

RL Security

Attacks against and defenses for reinforcement-learning agents, including backdoor and poisoning threats that surface only under specific deployment conditions.

  • SleeperNets
  • DAZE
  • Q-Incept
